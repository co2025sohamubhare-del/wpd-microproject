About Our College

Vivekanand Education Society is a well known technical institution focused on providing quality education in engineering and technology. The college encourages innovation, practical learning, and professional development. With experienced faculty, modern laboratories, and strong industry connections, the institution aims to develop skilled engineers and responsible citizens who can contribute to society.

Admissions

Admissions are open for various engineering and technical programs. Students can apply through the official admission portal or contact the college administration for further guidance regarding eligibility criteria, courses, and admission procedures.

Our Faculty

The college has highly qualified and experienced faculty members who focus on both theoretical and practical learning. Faculty members guide students in academics, research, and innovation to help them achieve their career goals.
